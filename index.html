Dr. Kendall Ackley

Monash University · Melbourne, Australia · Gravitational Wave Astrophysicist ·

I am a postdoctoral research fellow at the School of Physics & Astronomy at Monash University.

I received my PhD from the University of Florida in 2017 with the title "A realistic end-to-end analysis: An optimized procedure for finding elusive electromagnetic counterparts to gravitational wave events" under joint supervision of Prof. Stephen S. Eikenberry and Prof. Sergey Klimenko.

Since then I have moved to Melbourne Australia to work with the GOTO observatory and with The ARC Centre of Excellence for Gravitational Wave Discovery (OzGrav) working on electro-magnetic follow-up of gravitational-wave events.
