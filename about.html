I am currently a postdoctoral research fellow at the School of Physics & Astronomy at Monash University. I work primarily with the GOTO observatory I received my PhD from the University of Florida in 2017 with the title "A realistic end-to-end analysis: An optimized procedure for finding elusive electromagnetic counterparts to gravitational wave events" under joint supervision of Sergey Klimenko and Stephen S. Eikenberry.
Since then I have moved to Melbourne Australia to work with GOTO as a member of OzGrav working on electro-magnetic follow-up of gravitational-wave events.
